Insu Yun is a PhD student at Georgia Institute of Technology. He is interested
in system security in general, especially, binary analysis, automatic
vulnerability detection, and applied cryptography.

In addition to research, he has been participating in several capture-the-flag
(CTF). In particular, he received the Black Badge from DEF CON as the winner
in 2015 (DEFKOR) and 2018 (DEFKOR00t).

Prior to joining Georgia Tech, he received his BS degree in Computer Science
from KAIST in 2010.
