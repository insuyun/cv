Insu Yun is a Ph.D. student at Georgia Institute of Technology. He is
interested in system security in general, especially, binary analysis,
automatic vulnerability detection, and applied cryptography.

In addition to research, he has been participating in several hacking
competitions. In particular, he received the Black Badge from DEF CON as the
winner in 2015 (DEFKOR) and 2018 (DEFKOR00t).

Prior to joining Georgia Tech, he received his BS degree in Computer Science
from KAIST in 2010.
